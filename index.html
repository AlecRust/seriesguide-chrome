seriesguide-chrome

A chrome plugin to manage your favorite tv-shows and their schedules with automagic ThePirateBay integration

Installation:

  • download the .crx file
  • Open your chrome extensions tab
  • drag the crx to the extensions tab to install

Add your favorite series to the list and then click one to view the schedule. Behind each episode that's aired is a magnet link. Click that to automatically check thepiratebay for the torrent with the most seeds for SE. Click the magnet link behind a torrent to download it.
